Hands IN Piano
  • Time：06.12.2021 1900p.m.
  • Where：Student Activity Center Lecture hall A101
Introduction
  Music isn’t just music, it’s life, it’s got a heartbeat. With the reach of the black and white keys, we can create indefinite melodies.
  Finally after 4 years, NDHU Piano Club wants to hold another performance-only grand event including piano solos, duets, and ensemble (with violin, cello and piano).
  Due to COVID 19, you may feel like you’re missing out when unable to attend these concerts. If you feel like this, you might as well attend our final performance.
  The black and white keys are interlaced with different tones. Whether it’s classical or pop, as long as you let your fingers run freely on the keyboards, the notes will flow out from your hands and the melody will forever be engraved in people’s hearts.
